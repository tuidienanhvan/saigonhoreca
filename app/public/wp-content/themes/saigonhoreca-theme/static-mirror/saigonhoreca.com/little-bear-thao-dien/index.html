The design concept of Little Bear

The kitchen design of Little Bear is a fusion of minimalism, modernity, and sophistication. Saigon Horeca used mainly stainless steel materials, making Little Bear’s kitchen a professional and convenient workspace for chefs.

Little Bear's Challenge and Saigon Horeca's Solution:

Little Bear’s requirements:

  • Utilize a square kitchen space with limited area.
  • Ensure the kitchen has full functionality for both European and Asian cuisine.
  • Provide a spacious working area for chefs to unleash their creativity.

Within the limited canvas of Little Bear’s square kitchen, Saigon Horeca orchestrated a symphony of functionality and flair. Industrial regulations danced seamlessly with culinary freedom, thanks to meticulously designed ventilation, optimized storage, and an ergonomic layout that empowered chefs. Every detail, from gleaming stainless steel to strategically placed shelves, whispered efficiency. More than just a workspace, this kitchen transcended the mundane, becoming an incubator for culinary artistry. Innovation and experimentation found fertile ground amidst the rhythmic clanging of pans and the vibrant hum of activity. Little Bear’s kitchen, infused with Saigon Horeca’s expertise, wasn’t just an operational necessity; it was a testament to their commitment to crafting spaces that nurture culinary excellence, bite by exquisite bite.

Saigon Horeca's solution:

  • Centralized storage and processing:
    • Two back-to-back refrigerated drawer tables to maximize space utilization and optimize fresh food storage.
    • Cabinets, sinks, and shelves for pre-processing equipment.
  • Cooking area:
    • Located on the left side of the workspace, symbolizing the warmth and passion of the kitchen.
    • Equipped with high-quality cooking appliances to create delicious dishes.

Saigon Horeca's DNA is evident in the artistic design of the bar counter

  • Plating and bar counter:
    • Open bar counter directly opposite the cooking area, allowing diners to witness the chefs’ culinary artistry.
    • Provides a unique and engaging dining experience.
  • Optimized storage:
    • Large refrigerators and freezers on the left wing of the kitchen ensure freshness of ingredients.
    • Utilizes renowned brands such as Fujimak and Hoshizaki for reliability and performance.
  • Custom-made stainless steel worktops and wall shelves:
    • Designed specifically for Little Bear to maximize space and functionality.

Saigon Horeca’s attention to detail and commitment to excellence resulted in a kitchen that not only meets Little Bear’s requirements but also exceeds their expectations.
