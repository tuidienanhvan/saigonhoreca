If you feel overwhelmed by the busy urban life and want to find a cool, fresh place to relax, dine, or chat with friends, come to The Brix.

Located in the heart of District 2, The Brix restaurant is designed as a tropical resort island. It features a refreshing swimming pool and a lush, vibrant forest area.

Materials such as ceiling wood and foundation tiles were chosen in the design based on traditional Vietnamese materials, taking the country‘s industrial period as an idea.

Inside the restaurant, there is also a Momentum Living exhibition room, with a minimalist design following a classic trend. The harmonious combination of bright and dark colors creates an elegant and sophisticated ambiance.

At The Brix, diners not only enjoy the tranquility of nature but also indulge in the excellent European-style cuisine, contributing to a complete and fulfilling dining experience.

The refreshing and serene mood of the guests is partly contributed by Saigon Horeca, the consulting and equipment provider for the Kitchen and Pastry areas of the restaurant, supporting the chefs in creating delicious dishes that ignite every conversation.

Main Kitchen Area

The cooking area is an important space in most restaurants, and the interior design should ensure convenience and comfort, allowing chefs to unleash their creativity in culinary arts.

From the receiving and pre-processing area to storage, cooking, plating, and dishwashing, we have arranged each section according to food safety standards, making it convenient for staff during operations.

The food preparation and storage area includes sinks, refrigerators, freezers, and wall-mounted shelves.

The main kitchen area features a deep fryer, a 6-burner stove, a griddle, an oven, and an exhaust hood.

The dishwashing area is designed in accordance with the process from the coated spray basin table with litter holes to the washing machine, clean dish table, dish face down shelves,…

The design incorporates natural lighting through a sealed roof, providing a pleasant atmosphere and avoiding the stuffiness of a closed space.

Pastry Area

In the 18-square-meter space, we have carefully planned and optimized the layout of the pastry equipment, ensuring the smooth process from kneading and shaping dough to proofing, storing, and baking, resulting in aromatic and skillfully crafted pastries by the hands of the chefs, with the assistance of specialized equipment provided by Saigon Horeca.

The pastry equipment at The Brix includes ingredient storage cabinets, display refrigerators, dough sheeters, pastry ovens, and other specialized stainless-steel appliances.
